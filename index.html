About

I made this project to practice some javascript. My end goal is to have a system where you can invite a friend and play a game of battle ship together. Right now I am still working on the logic of everything.

Controls

  • Left click on player ones blue board to place a ship.
  • Press R to rotate the piece.
  • Click on player one's white board to take a guess.
  • If you click all the correct spots on player one's guess board (the white board) the win condition will play out.
  • Press W to simulate a win condition.
